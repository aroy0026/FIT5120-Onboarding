General Protection Tips

Use SPF30+ or higher Apply sunscreen before going outside and reapply it regularly, especially during stronger UV conditions.
Wear protective clothing Choose clothing that covers more skin, and add a hat and sunglasses when UV levels are high.
Stay in shade Try to reduce direct sun exposure during the strongest part of the day, especially near peak UV time.
